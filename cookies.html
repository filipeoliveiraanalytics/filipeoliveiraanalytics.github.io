Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-24

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites work correctly and can be used for analytics.

2. Cookies we use

This website uses the following categories:

Essential

These cookies are required to remember your cookie choice. They are stored as local site storage using localStorage:

  • fo_cookie_consent – stores your analytics consent choice.

Analytics (Google Analytics 4)

If you accept analytics cookies, we activate Google Analytics 4 to understand usage and improve the site. Typical Google Analytics cookies include:

  • _ga – distinguishes users.
  • _ga_* – persists session state.

Analytics cookies are only set after your consent via the cookie banner.

3. How to change your choice

You can withdraw consent by clearing site data in your browser (cookies/site storage) for this website, then reloading the page to see the banner again.

4. Third parties

Google processes analytics data according to its policies: Google Privacy Policy.
