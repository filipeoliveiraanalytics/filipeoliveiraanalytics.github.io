Featured Project – F1 2025 Season

This Power BI report explores the entire 2025 Formula 1 season with a TV-style design. It includes a season overview, race deep dives and an interactive title-fight scenario where users can simulate the final races and see who becomes World Champion.

  • Custom data model built from APIs and web scraping.
  • Season overview with driver & constructor standings, cumulative points and performance scatterplots.
  • Race detail page with grid vs finish analysis and position-change visuals.
  • Title fight scenario simulator powered by DAX measures and what-if parameters.
