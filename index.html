Featured project F1 2025 Season

This Power BI report explores the entire 2025 Formula 1 season with a TV-style design and an interactive title-fight simulator where you can tweak race results and see who becomes World Champion.

  • Season overview with driver & constructor standings, cumulative points and performance scatterplots.
