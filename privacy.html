This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is handled when you visit https://filipeoliveiraanalytics.com/ (“the website”).

The website is operated by Filipe Oliveira Analytics. Contact: filipe@filipeoliveiraanalytics.com.

This is a portfolio website. We do not offer account creation or contact forms. We may process limited technical and usage data for analytics and security purposes, such as:

  • Pages visited and interactions (e.g., clicks), aggregated for reporting.

3. Purpose and Legal Basis

We use analytics to understand website performance and improve content. The legal basis is your consent (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) for non-essential analytics cookies.

4. Google Analytics (GA4)

This website uses Google Analytics 4, provided by Google LLC, to measure website usage. Google Analytics uses cookies to generate statistics and reports. Analytics cookies are only set and Google Analytics is only activated after you provide consent via the cookie banner.

We configure GA to avoid advertising features for this portfolio (e.g., Google Signals / ad personalization disabled where applicable). Learn more about Google’s privacy practices: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Analytics data is processed by Google as a service provider.

6. Retention

Analytics data retention depends on the settings in Google Analytics. We keep data no longer than necessary for analysis.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or object to processing. You can also withdraw consent at any time by changing your cookie preferences (clear site storage or reset cookies). Contact: filipe@filipeoliveiraanalytics.com.
